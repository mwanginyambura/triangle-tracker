The different types of a triangle.

EQUILATERAL TRIANGLE.

It is a figure where all dimensions are equal.

  • Has 3 sides
  • Are the only regular polygon with three sides, and appear in a variety of contexts

ISOSCELES TRIANGLE

The figure has two dimensions equal and the other side different from the rest.

SCALENE TRIANGLE.

All sides of the figure are not equal.
